Bread Head ecosystem

Right now it’s an app. But the vision is a full platform: classroom-ready curriculum, workshops, and resources for schools and families.

  • Student app: Lessons, budget sim, journal.
  • Teacher kit (in progress): Slides, activities, assessments.
  • Workshops: Small-group sessions on real teen money problems.
  • Resource library: Guides, templates, and “first budget” checklists.

Start where you need the most help.

Every part of Bread Head connects, but you don’t have to use everything at once. Use it like a dashboard: jump into the piece that matters today.

1 · Lessons

Understand the “why.”

Structured slides that explain the core ideas: budgeting, saving, credit, debt, investing basics, and more.

  • Short, focused units
  • Teen scenarios, not textbook filler
  • Progress you can see

Practice without consequences.

Use a fake-but-realistic budget to test choices before you make them with actual money.

  • Try different incomes and expenses
  • See where money leaks out
  • Assign every dollar a job

  • Turn “I don’t know” into data
